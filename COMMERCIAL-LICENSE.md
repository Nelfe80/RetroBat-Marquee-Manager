# APIExpose Commercial Reseller License

## Purpose

This document defines the commercial licensing rules for APIExpose.

RetroBat remains free software. This commercial license only concerns APIExpose and its associated data pack.

## Commercial use

A commercial license is required when APIExpose is included, preinstalled, bundled, configured, advertised or used as part of any paid offer.

Commercial use includes, but is not limited to:

- selling a RetroBat-based arcade cabinet including APIExpose;
- selling a mini-PC including APIExpose;
- selling a hard drive, SSD, SD card or USB drive including APIExpose;
- selling a digital pack including APIExpose;
- selling a preconfigured RetroBat installation including APIExpose;
- offering paid installation or paid support where APIExpose is included or configured;
- using APIExpose as a selling argument for a commercial product.

## License types

### Reseller unit license

One commercial unit sold to a third party requires one reseller unit license.

```text
APX-RSU-2026-000184
```

### Reseller batch license

A reseller batch license allows a reseller to purchase a batch of commercial units.

A batch license alone does not authorize unlimited copies. Each sold unit must have a unique unit certificate or equivalent license identifier.

```text
APX-RSB-2026-000042
APX-CERT-2026-000018
```

## License display

Every commercial copy of APIExpose must be able to display or expose:

- the license type;
- the unique license identifier;
- the reseller name or reseller identifier when applicable;
- the commercial license status;
- the notice that RetroBat is free software.

## No hiding or bypassing

In any commercial distribution, it is forbidden to remove, hide, rename, disable, modify or bypass:

- license notices;
- attribution notices;
- authenticity status;
- reseller license status;
- license verification logic;
- API endpoints exposing license information.

## Personal licenses are not commercial licenses

A personal APIExpose license is not a reseller license.

A personal license may not be copied, duplicated, preinstalled, bundled, redistributed or sold to a third party.

## Per-unit principle

Unless otherwise agreed in writing:

```text
1 commercial machine sold = 1 APIExpose reseller license
```

or:

```text
1 commercial copy distributed = 1 APIExpose reseller license
```

Commercial redistribution is only authorized under a separate written agreement issued by the APIExpose rights holder.
